			<h1 align="justify">Internet of Things Laboratory @ RI</h1>
			<p>
				Rajalakshmi Institutions IOT Lab provides opportunities for dynamic minds to perform R&D
				in the field of wireless and embedded devices that are connected across internet.
				IoT involves communication of a wide range of devices through internet to perform
				certain predefined functionalities, that may include vehicle monitoring, agriculture monitoring,
				security surveillance, telemedicine and smart cities. Our IoT lab at Rajalakshmi
				aims at helping the nation in achieving the vision of smart cities by developing conceptual ideas
				through studentâ€™s innovative ideas and research.
			</p>
			<h2>Mission</h2>
			<p>
				<ul>
					<li>The Internet of things lab at REC aims to identity dynamic minds, who could implement concepts into working model.</li>
			  	<li>To undertake challenging tasks and deliver low-cost solutions for enabling a good communication network among pervasive devices.</li>
					<li> To enable various networking infrastructure to communicate with tiny devices to prove that IOT is here for the future generation.</li>
				</ul>
			</p>
			<h2>Road Map</h2>
				<h3>Year 1-2</h3>
					<ul>
						<li> To gain understanding in this future generation field by experimenting basic devices to work with the environment.</li>
						<li>To study the potential of Intel boards and other open source environments and deliver it to educate students.</li>
						<li>To focus on scholars and students to execute their research works for day to day problems.</li>
					</ul>
				<h3>Year 2-3</h3>
					<ul>
						<li> To Enable students and faculty to publish their research activities done in the lab.</li>
						<li>To undertake consultation work with startups and deploy their data sets to gain funds.</li>
						<li>To undertake training activities from IT majors.</li>
					</ul>
				<h3>Year 3-5</h3>
					<ul>
						<li> To propose reliable solutions to real world problems and market them. </li>
						<li> To analysis the forecasting, and the simulation of complex socio-economical problems and provide it for government agencies for embedding into smart city solution.</li>
					</ul>
