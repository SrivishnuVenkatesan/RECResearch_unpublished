			<h1 align="justify">Mobile Applications Development @ RI</h1>
			<p>
				Mobile Development Lab provides unique environment for experimental research in the field
				of developing app and app based products. The lab helps in identifying the potential of student
				who is competent  in programming android, windows and iPhones.
			</p>
			<h2>Mission</h2>
			<p>
				<ul>
					<li>To provide resources for students to become more competitive in the workplace
						by gaining exposure to the power of apps as well as experience
						in the various aspects of the process of defining, designing and testing apps
						in a cross-disciplinary and innovative learning environment.</li>
			  	<li>To establish a center of software development for mobile devices and carter to the needs of young generations.</li>
					<li>To undertake challenging tasks and deliver low-cost solutions future generation.</li>
				</ul>
			</p>
			<h2>Road Map</h2>
				<h3>Year 1-2</h3>
					<ul>
						<li>To create a clubhouse environment for student developers to share knowledge and ideas and pursue collaborative projects.</li>
						<li>Periodic public apps-related events, including developer workshops, panel sessions, and student showcases.</li>
						<li>To Support opportunities for student research and development projects.</li>
					</ul>
				<h3>Year 2-3</h3>
					<ul>
						<li> To Enable students and faculty to publish their research activities done in the lab.</li>
						<li>To undertake consultation work with startups to gain funds.</li>
						<li>To undertake training activities from IT majors.</li>
					</ul>
				<h3>Year 3-5</h3>
					<ul>
						<li>To propose reliable solutions to real world problems and market them.</li>
						<li>To design and develop native apps in iPhone, iPad, Android and mobile platforms.
							We help businesses create mobile capabilities for their internal business as well as their customer base.</li>
					</ul>

			</p>
