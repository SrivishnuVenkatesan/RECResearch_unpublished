			<h1 align="justify">Cloud Laboratory @ RI</h1>
			<p>
				Rajalakshmi Institution aims to provide flexible, scientific infrastructure
				for research on the future of cloud computing to build our own clouds,
				experimenting with new architectures that will form the basis for the next generation of computing platforms.
				It is expected that Cloud Computing will help in pooling of computing resources of Government Departments
				into large clouds thereby increasing utilization of computing resources effectively.
				It is built for running experiments that will lead to, or to a deeper understanding of the fundamentals of cloud computing.
			</p>
			<h2>Mission</h2>
			<p>
				<ul>
					<li>The mission of cloud lab is to identify new capabilities in future clouds for high performance computing.</li>
			  	<li> RCLs to conduct fundamental and applied research on all aspects of systems that integrate computing and information processing.</li>
					<li> To deliver virtual machines for remote access and reduce the cost of implementation of remote lab.</li>
					<li>To establish center of excellence through providing computing resources for small and medium enterprises and establish a business value.</li>
				</ul>
			</p>
			<h2>Road Map</h2>
				<h3>Year 1-2</h3>
					<ul>
						<li>Strengthen our faculty and students in utilizing the resource of cloud lab.</li>
						<li>Coordinates talks and trainings focused on cloud computing services such as Amazon Web Services (AWS), Microsoft Azure, and Google Cloud Platform.</li>
						<li>Enable scholars and students to compute their research works. </li>
					</ul>
				<h3>Year 2-3</h3>
					<ul>
						<li>To Enable students and faculty to publish their research activities done in the lab.</li>
						<li>To expand the cluster from a 4 node to 20 node and apply for fundingâ€™s to undertake research projects.</li>
						<li>To assist in deploying private cloud setup in other institutions.</li>
					</ul>
				<h3>Year 3-5</h3>
					<ul>
						<li>To develop scientific solutions for government projects and gain resources and funding.</li>
						<li>To make the cloud lab setup to perform better and increase infrastructure on par with entry level supercomputing facility.</li>
					</ul>
