			<h1 align="justify">Artificial Intelligence @ RI</h1>
			<p>
				The Artificial Intelligence Laboratory (AI Lab) is designed
				to facilitate an intellectual home for researchers and interested students
				from the Faculty of Organization and Informatics whose main research focuses on
				various fields of AI.
			</p>
			<h2>Mission</h2>
			<p>
				<ul>
					<li>To conduct cutting-edge, long-term standalone and collaborative research
						as well as education in artificial intelligence, multi-agent systems,
						robotics, computational intelligence, web and data mining, semantic web and related topics
						with a sharp focus on transferring knowledge into practice.
			  	<li> To develop machine learning algorithms using prediction methods.
				</ul>
			</p>
			<h2>Road Map</h2>
				<h3>Year 1-2</h3>
					<ul>
						<li>With mathematical research, instigate important strategies in AI.</li>
						<li>Gathering training data to solve complex problems.</li>
						<li>Design a model, smart enough to meet programmer's goal.</li>
					</ul>
				<h3>Year 2-3</h3>
					<ul>
						<li>To enable students and faculty to publish their research activities done in the lab.</li>
						<li>To undertake consultation work with startups to gain funds.</li>
						<li>To build valid decision criteria for uncertain problems.</li>
					</ul>
				<h3>Year 3-5</h3>
					<ul>
						<li>To design software agents for world-models.</li>
						<li>To develop techniques for the synthesis, integration and combination of decision procedures for a variety of problems.</li>
					</ul>

			</p>
